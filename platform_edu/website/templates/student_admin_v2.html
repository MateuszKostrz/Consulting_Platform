Student Rulebook & Guidelines

A couple of important rules for your upcoming classes.

General
Get help on issues related to general rules

If a class is cancelled on the day it is scheduled, the student will be charged for the class, unless it is possible for the tutor to reschedule.

If a class is cancelled last minute due to tutor's unavailability, then that class will cancel out with any missed class caused by the student. If not possible, a refund will be paid to the student.

The only piece of equipment a student needs is a laptop, stable internet connection and (preferably) a webcam.

All students receive full unlimited access to {{ site_name }} for free, for the time when classes take place.
Referrals & Discounts
Learn about bulk discounts and referral possibilities.

One free class is given for each successful referral, so for a student that completes a trial class and continues.

When 2 or more hours per week are purchased, a 10% discount is applied to the total price.

For 2–4 people groups, a 30–50% discount per person is applied.
Payments
Everything related to payments for your classes.

All classes are tracked by the tutors and logged into the admin panel. They are available for preview by students and parents at any time.

Payments are due at the start of each month. On the 5th of each month the system automatically sums up all classes. Payment links are sent by the 10th of each month.

Classes can be paid via invoices or payment links. Invoices are sent in GBP but can be paid in any currency based on the exchange rate at the time of payment.

Rules and Guidelines are accepted. If you have any questions, please contact us here.
